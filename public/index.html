Objectives

Showcase community products, traditional cuisines, and local crafts for commercialization at affordable prices.

Help rural communities through MADANI Sales while promoting unique local beach features as tourism attractions.

Festival Program

A rich program of activities designed for all ages and communities

Venue Setup

Festival Site Layout

Each festival location features a carefully designed layout including a main performance stage, VM2026 Pavilion with Arabian tent (20'x20'), exhibition tents for craftsmen, food vendor tents (10'x10'), Rahmah MADANI sales area, and family-friendly open spaces.

Main Performance Stage
VM2026 Pavilion & VIP Area
Exhibitor & Craft Tents
Food Vendor Area
Instagrammable Photo Spots
Neon Lighting & Decor
Food & Culture

Traditional Food Bazaar

Authentic Malaysian cuisine including nasi lemak, satay, laksa, keropok lekor, and viral street food. All at affordable Rahmah MADANI prices accessible to everyone.

Entertainment

Cultural Performances

Traditional Malaysian dances from various ethnic groups. The diversity of cultural performances celebrates unity and promotes the beauty of Malaysian heritage to all communities.

Art & Craft

Handicraft Demonstrations

Watch artisans create traditional Malaysian crafts live. Visitors can see the craft-making process firsthand, purchase unique handmade souvenirs, and support local entrepreneurs.

Sports

Fun Run Cuti-Cuti Malaysia

Beachside fun run with local delicacy checkpoints featuring cekodok, keropok lekor, pisang goreng, and kuih. Participants get goodie bags with VM2026 merchandise and prizes for traditional costume categories.

Shopping

Rahmah MADANI Sales

Affordable goods and necessities at festival-special prices. Affordable travel packages from tourism operators for all communities, especially suburban and B40 groups.

Entertainment

Magic Shows & Fire Performances

Spectacular magic shows and mesmerizing fire-eating performances on the beach at sunset. Featuring renowned performers creating unforgettable beachside entertainment.

Live Music

Port Buskers & Local Bands

Live acoustic performances from popular Malaysian buskers plus talented local bands from each state, creating the perfect beachside atmosphere.

Photo Spots

Instagrammable Attractions

Eye-catching welcome arches, giant #VM2026 letter installations on the beach, and neon light decorations including palm trees and flamingos - perfect for social media moments.

Kids & Family

Traditional Games & Kids Activities

Face painting, balloon art with VM2026 themes, congkak, dam ular (snakes & ladders), and coloring contests. Classic Malaysian childhood games that bring families together.

Sukan Rakyat

Community Sports

Tug of war, tarik upih, lumba tempurung (coconut shell race), sack race, coconut bowling, and stilts race. Traditional village sports that bring communities together in friendly competition.

Kids Zone

Soopa Doopa Kids Zone

Bouncy castles and inflatable play areas for children, creating a family-friendly festive atmosphere. A safe and fun space where kids can play while parents enjoy the festival.

Coffee Corner

Kopi-Kopi Korner

A relaxed coffee corner for visitors to unwind and enjoy local coffee varieties. Sponsors: ZUS Coffee, Alicafe, Old Town White Coffee, Oriental White Coffee, Wonda Coffee.

Competition

Kampung Cooking Competition

Traditional village cooking contest showcasing heritage recipes passed down through generations. Sponsors: Saji, Adabi, Rempah Mak Siti, MYDIN.

Competition

Village Barista Competition

Local coffee barista competition highlighting unique local coffee culture and brewing traditions. Sponsors: ZUS Coffee, Alicafe, Old Town White Coffee.

Mascots

Mascots Wira & Manja

Sun bear mascots that entertain visitors, especially children, and promote Visit Malaysia 2026 via social media sharing and photo opportunities throughout the festival.

Promotion Plan

Multi-channel promotional strategy to maximize festival reach and awareness

RTM Radio Outside Broadcast

State RTM radio live broadcast from each festival location, providing real-time coverage and driving visitor turnout

Banner & Bunting

Physical promotion with banners and bunting around the festival venue and main roads leading to each beach location

Digital Media

Social media promotion across Facebook, X (Twitter), TikTok, and YouTube with engaging festival content and highlights

Influencers

Promotion through social media influencers and content creators to reach younger demographics and drive viral engagement
