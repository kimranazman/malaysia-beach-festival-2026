02 — Objectives

Why This Festival?

01

Support Visit Malaysia 2026

Encourage domestic tourism and inspire Malaysians to explore their own country's beautiful coastlines through the national campaign.

02

Affordable Tourism for All

Provide accessible travel packages and affordable pricing targeting suburban communities and B40 groups through Rahmah MADANI initiative.

03

Promote Local Heritage

Showcase community products, traditional cuisines, and local handicrafts for commercialization at affordable prices supporting local entrepreneurs.

04

Unique Beach Attractions

Help rural communities through MADANI Sales while promoting each unique local beach as a distinctive tourism attraction.

04 — Activities

What's Happening

Food & Culture Traditional Food Bazaar

Nasi lemak, satay, laksa, keropok lekor — all at Rahmah MADANI affordable prices.

Entertainment Cultural Performances

Traditional dances from various ethnic groups celebrating Malaysia's cultural diversity.

Art & Craft Handicraft Village

Watch artisans create traditional crafts live and take home unique handmade souvenirs.

Sports Fun Run

Beachside fun run with local delicacy checkpoints — cekodok, keropok lekor, kuih.

Shopping Rahmah Sales

Affordable goods and travel packages for all communities, especially B40 groups.

Image Prompt:

Fire dancers and magic show performers on a tropical beach at night with neon palm tree lights.

Shows Magic & Fire Performances

Spectacular magic shows and fire-eating acts plus live busker performances at sunset.

05 — Facilities

Venue & Infrastructure

VM2026 Pavilion

20'x20' Arabian tent with VM2026 branding, info counter, connected modular VIP lounge

Exhibitor Tents

A-frame tents with facade and decorations for exhibitors and handicraft vendors

Food Vendor Area
